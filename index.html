About Me

Hi, I'm Alex. I started writing code in high school after taking apart a broken laptop and realizing software was just another system I could understand. A decade later I've shipped products at startups and at scale — from real-time collaboration tools to billing infrastructure handling millions of requests a day.

What I care about: developer experience, performance budgets, and building UI that respects the user. I'm at my best when I'm bridging design and engineering, prototyping fast, and writing TypeScript that future-me won't dread.

When I'm not at the keyboard you'll find me bouldering, brewing pour-over coffee, or contributing to open source. Recent technologies I've been enjoying:

  • TypeScript
  • React & Next.js
  • Go
  • PostgreSQL
  • Tailwind CSS
  • Kubernetes
